Jyoti Fellowship

The Jyoti Fellowship program is organized by the Tata Steel Foundation from many years. This program is specially for the SC/ST student to support them financially for their study. Students who belong to SC/ ST category will be benefited from this scheme. Beneficiaries will get financial assistance to pursue their academics. This is the best opportunity for the students to complete their further education without taking the burden of financial issues. Student can fill the fellowship program form through this website and can give exam to get benefit. The organization is taking exam every year and giving the benefit through this program to the student who passed the exam.
